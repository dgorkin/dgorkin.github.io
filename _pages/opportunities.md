## Graduate students

Our ability to accept new PhD thesis students varies from semester to semester. Students who are already enrolled in Emory's [Graduate Division of Biological and Biomedical Sciences (GDBBS)](https://www.gs.emory.edu/academics/programs/biomedical-sciences.html), and who are interested in rotating in the lab, should contact Dr. Gorkin directly.

---

## Undergraduate students

Our ability to accept new undergraduate students varies from semester to semester. Students who are interested in joining the lab should register their interest using [this form](https://forms.gle/eo1SGsm3Z1cDhrqRA). Once you fill out the form, your interest will be registered, and Dr. Gorkin will be in touch if/when a potential opportunity arises.
